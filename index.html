Prophet Muhammad (pbuh)

The man who saved Trillions of lives

Here's a time line of Prophet Muhammad (pbuh)'s life:

  • 570 - Born in Mecca, Arabia.
  • 595 - Marries Khadijah, a wealthy and respected merchant, at the age of 25.
  • 610 - Receives the first revelation from Allah (God) through the Angel Gabriel while meditating in the Cave of Hira. This event marks the beginning of his prophethood.
  • 613 - Starts preaching the message of Islam publicly, calling people to worship one God and denouncing idol worship.
  • 622 - Emigrates to Medina, known as the Hijra, after facing persecution in Mecca. This event marks the beginning of the Islamic lunar calendar.
  • 624 - Leads the Muslims in the first major battle, the Battle of Badr, against the Quraysh of Mecca, achieving a significant victory.
  • 628 - Negotiates the Treaty of Hudaybiyyah, establishing a ten-year truce between the Muslims and the Quraysh.
  • 630 - Conquers Mecca peacefully and forgives his former enemies, demonstrating a message of mercy and compassion.
  • 632 - Performs his farewell pilgrimage (Hajj) in Mecca, where he delivers his final sermon, emphasizing the importance of unity and equality among Muslims.
  • 632 - Passes away in Medina at the age of 63. His death marks the end of his earthly life but the continuation of his legacy as the last Prophet of Islam.

"Indeed, you (Muhammad) are of a great moral character." (Quran 68:4)
